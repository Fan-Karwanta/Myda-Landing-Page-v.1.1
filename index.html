About

A golden ritual. Delivered to your door.

MYDA is a home luxury massage and wellness service designed for those who move through the world with purpose—and deserve a moment to pause. Inspired by the myth of Midas and the healing power of touch, we bring curated spa experiences directly into your home, transforming ordinary spaces into sanctuaries of calm and care.

Every treatment is delivered by highly trained therapists who understand that true luxury lies not in extravagance, but in intention. From the warmth of our linens to the scent of our oils, every detail is chosen to help you return to yourself.

Whether you need to unwind after a long day or reset your body and mind, MYDA offers personalized treatments that respect your time, your space, and your rhythm.

We are proudly offering our services across Abu Dhabi, with discreet, on-demand bookings that work around your schedule—no stress, no travel, no compromise.

Because wellness shouldn’t wait.

It’s not just self-care. It’s self-respect.
It’s a return to yourself, in gold.
